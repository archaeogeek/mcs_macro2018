What is Macro?

From the greek "MAKROS", meaning "long or large"

In photography, it tends to mean "small things really close up"

Technical bit

Macro photography generally refers to images with a reproduction ratio from 1:6 to 1:1.

Reproduction ratio refers to the ratio of the image on the camera sensor to the size of the object.

So knowing the reproduction ratio means knowing the size of the sensor on your camera.

My camera is an Olympus Pen Mini, with a sensor of 17.3x13mm, and my macro lens goes from 1:4 to 1:1, so I can technically fill the image with an object only 13mm tall!

Diopters can also be added to lens housings to increase the maginification further. This is equivalent to putting on reading glasses!

End of technical bit
